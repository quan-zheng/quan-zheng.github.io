About

Quan ZHENG is a postdoctoral researcher working with Prof. Matthias Zwicker at Department of Computer Science of University of Maryland, College Park (UMD). His research interests lie in visual computing, with special attention on efficient high-fidelity rendering and light transport simulation.

Quan Zheng obtained his Ph.D. degree from Institute of Software of Chinese Academy of Sciences (ISCAS). His advisor was Prof. Changwen Zheng. In 2012, He received the B.S. degree in Computer Science from Dalian University of Technology (DUT).

(How to pronounce ‘Quan Zheng’? It sounds like ‘Tsuan Chung’ in English.)
