About

Quan Zheng is a postdoc researcher in the graphics department of Max Planck Institute for Informatics led by Prof. Hans-Peter Seidel, where he is working with Gurprit Singh. Prior to moving to Saarbruecken, he was a postdoc in Prof. Matthias Zwicker's laboratory at University of Maryland, College Park. His research centers on visual computing, including efficient high-fidelity rendering, artificial intelligence, computational imaging, etc.

He received Ph.D. degree from Univerisy of Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2017, supervised by Prof. Changwen Zheng. Before that, he received B.E. of Computer Science from Dalian University of Technology in 2012. For contact, please send emails to qzheng(at)mpi-inf dot mpg dot de.

(Q: How to pronounce ‘Quan Zheng’? A: It sounds like ‘Tsuan Cheng’ in English.)
